Admissions to St. George School

Admissions Process

Our admissions process is designed to ensure that we enroll students who will thrive in our academic and social environment. We consider a variety of factors, including academic performance, standardized test scores, and personal qualities.

  1. Application Submission: Complete and submit the online application form.
  2. Document Submission: Provide all required documents, including transcripts and test scores.
  3. Interview: Schedule and attend an interview with our admissions team.
  4. Decision: Receive an admissions decision.
  5. Enrollment: Complete the enrollment process and submit the enrollment fee.

Required Documents

  • Completed Application Form
  • Official Transcripts
  • Standardized Test Scores (if applicable)
  • Letters of Recommendation
  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Passport-sized Photographs
